Privacy Policy — Mendikot

Last updated: June 2026

Mendikot ("the app") is a card game for playing with friends or bots. This page explains what information the app uses and how.

Information you provide

When you play, you enter a display name and pick an avatar. These are stored only in your device's local app storage and are shared with other players in the same game room for the duration of that room — they are not linked to any account or real identity.

Game statistics

Stats such as rounds played, wins, losses, and badges are stored only on your device (local storage) and are never transmitted to any server.

Multiplayer connectivity

To play online with others, the app connects to our game server to relay moves between players in the same room (card plays, scores, chat-free game state). This data exists only for the lifetime of the game room and is not stored permanently or sold to third parties.

No ads, no tracking, no analytics

The app does not show ads, does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs, and does not sell or share data with advertisers.

Permissions

The app requests internet access only, used to connect to the multiplayer game server.
