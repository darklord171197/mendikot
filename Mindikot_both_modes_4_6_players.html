Traditional Trump Flow

Trump is hidden at the start. When a player cannot follow the led suit, trump is declared. After that, trump is displayed on screen and trump cards beat other suits.

4-player mode uses all 52 cards. 6-player mode removes all 2s, so 48 cards are divided equally as 8 cards each.
